Easter Egg Scramble

The annual Easter scramble that was disrupted by the global pandemic, will resume this year to much fanfare. When asked, Mayor Greenwood said this, “This event always brings joy to our residents and is something we all look forward to. It is a wonderful opportunity for us to build a family friendly community and make lasting friendships and memories.” I couldn’t agree more. This event has always been something that my family and I have looked forward to participating in every year. We were so sad that we missed out on it the past few years. It’s wonderful that we can do it again.

For those who haven’t participated in the easter scramble before, this is a great year to join in the fun. As is tradition, the day starts out with pancakes in the park, followed by the easter egg hunt, starting at 10am. There are staggered times based on ages. The eggs are hidden ahead of time by city workers and volunteers. During and after the hunt, there will be face painting and various other easter related activities for the family to enjoy. So, please, come out and have fun with us this coming scramble. You won’t be disappointed.
