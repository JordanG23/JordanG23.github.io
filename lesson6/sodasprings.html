Spring Cleanup

It’s that time again! Get out your gloves and boots, spring has sprung, and spring cleanup is now upon us. As we all know the beauty of this area is in the natural surroundings. From the mountain peaks to the clear streams and geysers, there is much beauty to make you feel at peace here in this part of the world. The township would like to invite you to take part in our annual spring cleanup starting this Saturday. This includes also of course free pickup of yard waste and debris from your homes that has accumulated over the long winter months.

To kick off the cleanup, the town will be having a barbecue in the park. Prizes for the most waste collected by individuals, groups, and families will also be handed out at the conclusion of the Saturday’s festivities. Prizes have been generously donated by local sponsors. The cleanup is a great activity for our community and prepares our little town for the many visitors that will be coming through on their way to Yellowstone National Park, just across the border. This also promotes a feeling of unity within our town, as it allows each of us to serve one another in a fun way.
