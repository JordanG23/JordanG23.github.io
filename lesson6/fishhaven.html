Bear Lake Fishing Derby

Do you love to fish? Are you looking for a great opportunity and excuse to spend the day doing something you love? The Bear Lake fishing derby is gearing up for a great day of fishing. Come and enjoy the beautiful scenery surrounding this magnificent area. The fishing derby is open to anyone that wants to partake in this annual event. There will be prizes for the largest fish, most catches, and of course the ever-popular category of largest fish tale. The more loaded the story the better. How big was that fish?

All of this will take place on the pristine Bear Lake. Bear Lake is a beautiful azure lake, with plenty of room to cast your lines. The lake is 18 miles long by 7 miles wide, covering an area of 109 square miles. The lake is shared with the great state of Utah and welcomes many animal and human visitors throughout the year. It is said that the lake is 250,000 years old, and due to the fault subsidence, that still occurs to this day, the lake continues to deepen. The color of the lake is due in part to the presence of calcium carbonate in the water.
