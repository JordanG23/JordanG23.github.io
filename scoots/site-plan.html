Site Plan

Jordan Greenwood

Overview

Purpose

To provide a site for tourists to see what is available for rent at our two locations, and then reserve the scooter of their choice for the time they need it.

Target Audience

Tourists and locals alike, who desire to explore the beautiful countryside with a fun vehicle that will help them to feel the wind and sun. These will be people who have drivers licences and are old enough to operate a motorized vehicle safely.
