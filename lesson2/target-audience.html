The Target Audience of "Tomorrow's Weather"

Description of the Audience

    The target audience for "Tomorrow's Weather" will be youth all the way up to senior citizen. Targeting specifically those that live in the target region, but is still available to all who desire to visit the area.

  • An individual looking to see what is happening in their community.
  • Event Planners making sure they aren't overlapping with other events.
  • Parents planning their family outings.
  • Community leaders wanting to co-ordinate plans with the other communities in their area.
  • Someone wanting to know what the weather will be like in the days to come.
