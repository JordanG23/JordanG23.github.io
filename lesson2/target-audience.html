Target Audience

The Target Audience of "Tomorrow's Weather"


Description of the Audience

The target audience for "Tomorrow's Weather" will be youth all the way up to senior citizen. Targeting specifically those that live in the target region, but is still available to all who desire to visit the area. All manner of technology can be used to access this site. All income brackets will find it easy to access and navigate
