White Space

Topps Trading Cards

Topps.com

Topps makes great use of whitespace to display the items that they have for sale. The whitespace is also used to break up the different sections of their homepage. I quite enjoy the clean look and find it easy to navigate the site.

Visual Hierarchy

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

ChurchofJesusChrist.org

The church website uses Visual Hierarchy to draw the reader to the most important or latest articles that are published to the website. Any new announcements are displayed with an eyecatching color at the top of the page. I find it really easy to see what they would like me to focus on when I visit the site.

Fitt's Law

NFL

NFL.com

The NFL website makes use of Fitt's law by making their buttons easily seen and accessible. As I navigate through the site, I know where I need to press to look at an article or access another page on their site. I know what images are videos by the display of a play symbol in the middle of the image.
