Warm Spring Invites Tourists.

The month of March has brought warmth back to the Idaho region, particularly in the three towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Idaho is well known for having all four seasons present, and this year is no exception. Come and enjoy what Idaho has to offer. Explore the many State Parks and experience the vast beauty this beautiful state has to offer. For example the annual tulip festival that takes place in Preston. The amazing array of colors and fragrant air will astound you. While you are exploring, you will find the kind of hospitality that will make you want to put your house up for sale and move here. The folks here in these three towns are kind, warm, and inviting. Idaho is a great state, most famously known for its potatoes. However, there is so much more to take in here while you are visiting. The landscape for one, with its rolling foothills, high mountain peaks, beautiful rivers and vast agricultural land, what's not to like? We invite you to feel what those who have come to live here over the years have felt. A little slice of heaven in the United States of America known as Idaho.
