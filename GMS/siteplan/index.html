Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the "Tomorrow's Weather" website is to provide a one stop page for the community's events and news. It is a website that will help the
members of a specific region to plan for upcoming events by seeing what is planned and what the weather will be like for the day that the activity is planned for.
It will also provide information on the town for visitors who are planning a visit to the town. The site will be able to support responsive design techniques so as to
enable the site to be viewed on devices such as smartphones all the way up to desktop computer.
